About PC Shop

PC Shop is a local computer shop focused on providing affordable, reliable, and high-quality PC components and custom PC builds. We aim to help students, gamers, and professionals find the right computer solutions based on their needs and budget.

Our team values transparency, proper component selection, and customer education. We believe that understanding PC parts helps customers make smarter decisions when building or upgrading their computers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make PC building simple and accessible by offering honest guidance, quality products, and dependable service. We strive to build long-term trust with our customers through consistent support and fair pricing.
